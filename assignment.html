The Lion - King of the Jungle

Lions are powerful predators found mainly in Africa. They live in groups called prides and are known for their strength and leadership.

Male lions are easily recognized by their manes, while females are skilled hunters.

Elephants - Gentle Giants

Elephants are the largest land animals. They are known for their intelligence, memory, and strong social bonds.

They use their trunks for communication, feeding, and even expressing emotions.

Dolphins - Masters of the Ocean

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine animals known for their playful nature and communication skills.

They live in groups and often interact closely with humans.
